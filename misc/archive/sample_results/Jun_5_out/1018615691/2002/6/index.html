Youth Entrepreneurs at Hopeworks

Several Hopeworks youth have been interviewed about the business ventures they have initiated during their involvement with Hopeworks.

Here is a small excerpt:
"Adrian Polo said he has learned constantly since coming to Hopeworks three months ago. This 16-yearsold student has loved not only learning about computers, but also learning about business. He has had an interest in owning a store since he was a child. Mr. Polo has designed and printed his own business cards with Hopeworks help. The practical exposure has been critical. He recognizes that creating a business card is only a small part of owning a business, but the planning and implementation process was valuable. There is a difference between reading [about business] and doing it, Mr. Polo said."
