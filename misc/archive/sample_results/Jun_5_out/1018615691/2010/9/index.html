Donate

Hopeworks has employed over 255 youth and helped over 1,200 trainees on their path to earning a college degree. We need your help to continue to make it possible for us to provide these services to Camden's youth.

There are a number of ways that you can donate to Hopeworks, including one-time donations, recurring donations, and starting your own fundraising campaign. Find out more about how you can donate to Hopeworks and help us help Camden's youth.

The C.R.I.B.

Hopeworks has created a safe, respectful, and celebratory atmosphere with structured learning where youth can and do succeed. But this atmosphere is often missing at home, where the environment is often unsafe and unstable. That is why we've opened the C.R.I.B.

The C.R.I.B. -- the Community Responding In Belief -- meets this challenge by providing a place where the atmosphere and security of Hopeworks extends into the living sphere. Learn more about the C.R.I.B.
