Raspberry Pi Day at Hopeworks!

The Raspberry Pi is a low cost, credit-card sized computer that plugs into a computer monitor or TV, and uses a standard keyboard and mouse. It is a capable little device that enables people of all ages to explore computing, and to learn how to program in languages like Scratch and Python.

Apply for the Camden PreAccelerator now!

The Rutgers-Camden CoLab & Hopeworks N’ Camden PreAccelerator is designed for entrepreneurs building startups, who are at the pre-concept, pre-revenue, pre-product, pre-launch stage. One of the opportunities of the PreAccelerators is a possible place at regional leading accelerators and incubators. The program delivers support around business planning, Minimum Viable Product, identifying first sales, and preparing for the next stage of growth support, whether at an accelerator or incubator, initial seed funding, or non-equity based finance (grants and loans).
