About

This is where you tell everyone about your Alumni group and highlight what you're doing. 

If your Alumni group has chapters, consider adding subnations to allow them to branch out on their own. Learn more here.

If you're using the Aware theme, or a theme based on Aware: add the tag homepage to any page to have an excerpt of that page appear on your homepage.
