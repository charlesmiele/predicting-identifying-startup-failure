How Much More Could Your School Do With an Active & Engaged Community of Supporters?

According to a recent survey conducted by the Moran Group, 50 million high school graduates would give back to their school if asked!   If alumni giving to colleges and universities is any indication of the potential for philanthropic support, that could mean an additional $8.0 billion a year in funding for public schools. 

But graduates are not the only constituent group to engage – how about alumni parents and the greater community in which the school resides?  Imagine them all coming together as a community or a Nation of enthusiastic advocates for the school in the public arena and by providing additional resources to create a margin of excellence for activities in the classroom, on the stage, and on the fields of competition.

To that end, Alumni Nations partners with public schools to grow strong, sustainable connections with graduates, alumni parents and community members that lead to increased levels of support, advocacy, engagement and volunteerism.  Alumni Nations helps schools connect with their graduates and the community in powerful ways for decades after graduation. 

Specifically, Alumni Nations:

1. Identifies and Engages Supporters.  We help find, connect, enroll and sustain an active community of external stakeholders.  We develop a database of potential members.

2.Manages Relationships with Constituents.  We upload the contact information into a customer relationship management system (CRM) so that you can start communicating with the members of your nation immediately.

3.Creates a Branded Web Presence.  We provide a customizable web-platform that creates opportunities to engage members of your community through multiple social media channels.

4.Identifies and Provides Additional Opportunities for Revenue.  We identify opportunities for additional revenue through donations, affinity programs and e-commerce solutions for the sale of products and services to members of your nation.

5.Provides fundraising consulting and campaign management.  For those schools considering major capital or endowment campaigns, we provide fundraising counsel and campaign management.
