About TransCorp Spine

Transcorp Spine is a privately held early stage company dedicated to developing unique, commercially viable, minimally invasive, and tissue saving products and procedures for the treatment of compressions in the Cervical Spine.

Transcorp Spine was founded by surgeons and engineers to address the needs of surgeons and patients.

Transcorp products are designed to enable the surgeon to rapidly, safely and effectively decompress the cervical spine without the need to perform a Diskectomy, Fusion or Arthroplasty.
