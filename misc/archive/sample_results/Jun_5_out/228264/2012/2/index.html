About TransCorp Spine

TransCorp Spine was founded by spine surgeons and engineers to address the surgical needs of both surgeons and patients in striving for better procedural outcomes and an early return to life activities (SM). We are a privately-held, early-stage, medical device company dedicated to developing unique, commercially-viable, minimally-invasive and tissue-saving products and procedures for the treatment of spinal pathologies.

Technology

TransCorp Spine products are designed to enable the surgeon to rapidly, safely and effectively perform minimally-invasive spinal procedures through a unique approach to correcting spinal pathologies. The TransCorp Spine products offer an excellent value proposition to the patient, to the surgeon, to the hospital and to the insurer, while offering a minimally-invasive surgical approach with innovative instruments and implants that provide for a rapid "return to normal life"(SM) for the patient.
