 Life Back in Motion 

Welcome to TransCorp Spine!

Working with Spine surgeons to set life back in motion!

Technology

TransCorp Spine products are designed to enable surgeons to rapidly, safely and effectively perform minimally-invasive spinal procedures through a unique approach to correcting spinal pathologies.

The TransCorp Spine products offer an excellent value proposition to the patient, surgeon, hospital and insurer, while providing a minimally-invasive surgical approach with innovative instruments and implants to set life back in motion for the patient.

Our Company

TransCorp Spine is a privately-held, early-stage, medical device company dedicated to developing unique, commercially-viable, minimally-invasive and tissue-saving products and procedures for the treatment of spinal pathologies.

The company was founded by spine surgeons and engineers to address the surgical needs of both surgeons and patients in striving for better procedural outcomes to set life back in motion.
