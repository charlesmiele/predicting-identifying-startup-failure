Welcome to Apploi!

Apploi is a community for job seekers and employers.
Job seekers can easily find and apply to jobs, get advice, tips, and training, and benefit from our community. 

Employers in turn can easily reach jobseekers on their computers and mobile devices, as well as via our network of kiosks.

With Apploi employers see personality – not just a piece of paper.
