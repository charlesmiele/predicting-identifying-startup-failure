Our Business is Designed to Bring Options to Yours

No one knows hardwoods like Northwest Hardwoods. We began in the Pacific Northwest with a single alder mill. Over the years, the company has steadily grown and expanded to become the largest manufacturer and global supplier of hardwood lumber, producing 14 species of domestic hardwood.  Today we import and distribute over 45 species of exotic hardwoods, and source both appearance and structural plywood.
