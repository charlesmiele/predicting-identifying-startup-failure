SEE OUR HARDWOODS AN EXPERIENCE DESIGNED AROUND YOU.
Hardwoods is an industry rooted in building trusting relationships. By focusing on each of our customers as our most important customer, we are able to learn and grow together every step of the way. Whether it’s species, supply, specialized production or logistics, we take the time to understand our customers’ needs so we can take care of them. That’s the foundation of trust. SEE OUR HARDWOODS Northwest Hardwoods began in the Pacific Northwest with a single alder mill. Over the years, the company has steadily grown and expanded to become one of the largest manufacturers of hardwood lumber in North America. Today, we offer virtually every species and grade of commercially viable North American hardwood lumber. We work with you to choose the best product for your needs. Why do so many customers continue do business with us? Because we care. As we’ve seen, the hardwoods industry can be difficult. When it is we are a rock-solid source of service and support.
