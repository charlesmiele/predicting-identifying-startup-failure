    Introducing SPion System™

    SPion System™ is a revolutionary mass spectrometry technology that defines a new architecture for the front end of a mass spectrometer. Empowered by the world’s first and only flexible ion guide technology for lossless ion transfer, SPion System™ seamlessly bridges the gap between sample and mass spectrometer.
