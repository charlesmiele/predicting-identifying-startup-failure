Context-aware discussions

Today, team conversations happen in email, wiki, CRM, and many other apps creating disconnected information silos. Important discussions are lost or left without much required attention.

300milligrams breaks those silos and brings all work related discussions into one place without losing their context. Each discussion thread is tied to a specific work artifact like document, customer account or milestone, allowing members to easily filter and identify discussions they should pay attention to.

Connected to your business tools

300milligrams connects to Basecamp, Dropbox, Google Docs and many other productivity and collaboration apps you already use with your team. You will see all documents, tasks, customer deals, wiki pages or any other relevant work data on a single dashboard allowing you to engage in a context-rich real-time discussion with your team mates.

Work related activity streams and real-time notifications

In addition to discussions 300milligrams tracks any changes to artifacts your team is working on. All important notification are gathered automatically, directly from your team collaboration tools, creating an instant opportunity to engage in a discussion over updated artifact.
