Based in New York City, Virtual Xperience LLC creates VR content for real estate, with a focus on residential and new development markets. Pre-sell or pre-lease your new developments with VR visualizations of the unbuilt environment.

Virtual Xperience provides digital products and services that help people explore and experience real estate in innovative and efficient ways. Our first-person, interactive 3D visualizations bring real estate professionals together with their clients more frequently and efficiently to close more deals.

Virtual Xperience solutions deliver a strong ROI to tech-savvy, top producing agents by increasing productivity with their clients, especially for pre-construction new developments.

Our technology helps clients go beyond photos, videos, 2D renderings, floor plans, blueprints, 360 tours and showings. Whether online or in the Oculus Rift, Virtual Xperience immerses users in new real estate experiences which are user-friendly, affordable, and challenge industry standards by delivering on the promise of virtual reality.
