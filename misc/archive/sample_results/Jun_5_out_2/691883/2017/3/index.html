Virtual Xperience Inc. (“VX”) accelerates the sales cycle for new developments, renovations, and pre-construction. VX clients pre-market their projects with immersive, photo-realistic VR visualizations across multiple mobile and VR platforms. Our digital products help people explore and experience real estate in innovative ways, making emotional connections with the unbuilt space when “walking through” and interacting with it. The VX experience helps people make well-informed decisions which maximize customer satisfaction.

VX creates a package of visually identical digital content across multiple platforms, including: traditional renderings, videos, 360* videos, mobile walkthroughs, and interactive VR experiences. VX brings real estate professionals together with their clients more frequently and efficiently to complete more transactions. With clients across the country and around the world, VX challenges industry standards by delivering on the promise of virtual reality as a business solution for the real estate industry.

Why VX:

Virtual Xperience creates VR content for real estate, with a focus on residential, commercial and new development markets. Pre-sell or pre-lease your new developments with VR visualizations of the unbuilt environment. Clients include home builders, multi family developers, real estate agents and brokers, interior designers, architects, owners, and more.

We provide digital products and services that help people explore and experience real estate in innovative and efficient ways. Our first-person, interactive 3D visualizations bring real estate professionals together with their clients more frequently and efficiently to close more deals.

Virtual Xperience solutions deliver a strong ROI to tech-savvy, top producing agents by increasing productivity with their clients, especially for pre-construction new developments.

Our technology helps clients go beyond photos, videos, 2D renderings, floor plans, blueprints, 360 tours and showings. Whether online, or in the Oculus Rift or HTC Vive, Virtual Xperience immerses users in new real estate experiences which are user-friendly, affordable, and challenge industry standards by delivering on the promise of virtual reality.
