Open Auto Transportation Trailer:
Open car transport trailer is the most common shipping method at an affordable price. It is an open top and side trailer that can vary in size from a 1 car flat hauler to an 11 car dual level hauler. Trailers that haul between 8 - 10 cars are the most common among our fleet and have many advantages that make it our most popular choice in vehicle transport among our customers.
Advantages: Less expensive, more readily available
Disadvantages: Vehicles are exposed to the elements of weather
Enclosed Car Shipping Trailer:
Our Enclosed Trailers are built with side walls and roof to protect your vehicles. These enclosed trailers also vary in size, we have enclosed trailers that can hold between 1-8 cars enclosed. Our Enclosed Trailers are typically used more for classic antique cars, exotics, high-end sport cars or vehicles with high dollar paint jobs. These factors are not necessary for shipping enclosed as it is also a matter of preference.
Advantages: Full protection from weather, road damage, and theft, you won't need to detail your vehicle when it is received
Disadvantages: More expensive, Width and Height restrictions for vehicles,
