What auto transport options does United Routes offer?

United Routes is a bonded and licensed car transport company by the Department of Transportation (license number MC#:650823) and offers fully secure open and enclosed shipping trailers.

Open Auto Transport:

Open auto transport has many advantages and is the most popular choice among our customers. Your car is transported in a trailer with an open top and sides and can vary in size from a 1-car flat hauler to an 11-car dual-level hauler. Trailers that haul 8-10 cars are the most common among our fleet. Open auto transport is readily available and very affordable.

Enclosed Car Transport:

Enclosed car transport trailers can hold 1-8 cars and offer your vehicle full protection from the weather and road damage, so you wont need to detail your vehicle when you pick it up. Customers with classic antique, exotic or high-end sport cars, or vehicles with expensive paint jobs, often choose enclosed car transport trailers. Enclosed car transport is more expensive than open auto transport, and width and height restrictions apply.
