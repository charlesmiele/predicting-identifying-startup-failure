Smart project decisions start here.

Join is the simplest way to get to the right decisions at the right time, for the right reasons — so your projects keep moving forward, no matter their size.

Decision history.
All in one place.

The Join construction decision platform tracks, monitors, and documents decisions — who, what, when, and why — ensuring project visibility and transparency while eliminating the possibility of data corruption. With Join, decision-makers have the data they need to make timely and smarter decisions. 

Learn More >

Decision impact, visualized. No number crunching skills required.

Join takes data from Excel and other estimating tools and provides an easier, consistent way to assess the impact of each preconstruction decision. With Join, you can ask “what if” questions and see the cost impact of an option in real-time. No need to be a spreadsheet wizard.  And no more worrying if you’re looking at the correct version or accidentally messing up a formula.

Project communication built for trust, collaboration, and accountability.

Construction projects require a lot of smart, experienced people working together to make smart decisions. And that means information sharing. Join provides tools that log all communication related to each decision and consolidate all relevant documents. No more scrolling and searching for emails. No more phone tags.
