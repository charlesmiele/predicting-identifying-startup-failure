Our Programs

Hopeworks offers two great programs involving technology training and job opportunities, coupled with academic support and formation. Our Day Training program is held Monday through Friday from 9:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m., and our program for in-school youth meets from 3:45 p.m to 6:00 p.m. Monday through Friday throughout the school year.

If you are interested in joining one of the top programs in the city, call (856-365-4673), stop by Hopeworks at 543 State St. in Camden, or fill out our program application.

The C.R.I.B.

Hopeworks has created a safe, respectful, and celebratory atmosphere with structured learning where youth can and do succeed. But this atmosphere is often missing at home, where the environment is often unsafe and unstable. That is why we've opened the C.R.I.B.

The C.R.I.B. -- the Community Responding In Belief -- meets this challenge by providing a place where the atmosphere and security of Hopeworks extends into the living sphere. Learn more about the C.R.I.B.
