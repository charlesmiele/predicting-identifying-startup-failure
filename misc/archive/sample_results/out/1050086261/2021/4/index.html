TUTR Humanity

TUTR was created so college students & tutors could connect on-demand, 24 hours a day. However, TUTR seeks to have a greater impact in Tuscaloosa. We aim to enrich our community by improving the lives of the people & our environment.


So that’s what we do.. we plant trees, we clean the community, we engage with the community & provide assistance anywhere we can.


Come Join Us! If you’re not a tutor or student.. no problem. Contact us here or through social media below. 


TUTR Humanity is comprised of two components; TUTR Tuscaloosa Cleanup & TUTR Tuscaloosa Nourishment
