Alumni Nations helps you to grow strong, sustainable connections with your graduates and community, providing a lifetime of support. These connections provide advocacy, engagement, talent, and funding opportunities. Alumni Nations helps your school to connect with your students and the community in powerful ways for decades after graduation. 

An Alumni Nation-Building System

  • Engaged Supporters- we help you to find, connect with and create active members
  • Enhanced Revenue – through donations and purchases from your nation
  • Expanded Resources – through volunteers, mentors, and community involvement

Alumni Nations community organizing process provides

  • Strategy development
  • Volunteer recruitment and training
  • Communication planning
  • Platform and engagement tools
  • Revenue drivers
  • On-going support
