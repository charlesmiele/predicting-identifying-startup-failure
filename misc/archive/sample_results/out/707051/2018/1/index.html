Frequently Asked Questions
Here you will find all the questions we get asked a lot. If you have additional questions you can contact us at hello@gettingles.com.
What do I get by joining Tingles?
By joining Tingles you get:
  • Earn revenue. Artists on Tingles earn 4x more per video view than on YouTube.
  • Get the best ASMR experience for your fans.
  • Get new fans on Tingles.
  • Get a 3Dio microphone for free when you reach 50 supporters.
How does it work?

Artists on Tingles earn revenue through user subscriptions. Watching videos on Tingles is free, but users can support you to get access to premium features. Users are able to choose between two support plans:

Gold plan (costs $4.99 a month)

Your gold supporters can:

  • download your videos for offline viewing
  • watch your exclusive videos and get early access to new videos
  • skip to next trigger in the same video (coming soon)
  • get everything from Silver plan
Silver plan (costs $1.99 a month)

Your silver supporters can:

  • get your wallpapers and other rewards for fans (we create these assets for you)
  • customize their home screen with your branded app icon

For Tingles users to start supporting you is just one tap. They don't need to create an account or add a credit card.

You receive 70% of the net-revenue and Tingles keeps 30% that goes into platform maintenance and development costs. Artists on Tingles earn 4x more per video view than on YouTube. And that's on top of their YouTube revenues.

What do I have to do?
Being on Tingles is no extra work for you. You don't need to upload videos. You keep posting your videos on YouTube as normal and they will also show on Tingles automatically. We encourage artists to create some exclusive videos for their Tingles supporters, but it's not a requirement. The main rewards for your supporters are provided by us.
How do I get a 3Dio microphone for free?
Tingles is partnering with 3Dio. As a Tingles partner you can get one 3Dio microphone for free when you reach enough supporters on Tingles:
  • For 50 Tingles supporters - get Base model Free Space for free (regular price: $499)
  • For 100 Tingles supporters - get Free Space XLR for free (regular price: $799)
  • For 200 Tingles supporters - get Free Space Pro II for free (regular price: $1,999)
How does Tingles help me grow my audience?
Tingles is a community of the most dedicated ASMR fans. Our average user watches 2 hours of ASMR a day. We promote your videos to our users who don't know you yet:
  • Your videos are featured in curated playlists
  • Your videos show in Home feed as recommended videos
  • Your profile is featured on top of Artists section in relevant countries
We also help you grow your Youtube channel. Tingles users are automatically subscribed to your YouTube channel when they start following you on Tingles if they have that feature turned on.
How is Tingles different than Patreon?
Tingles has three main advantages over Patreon: it offers a better experience for your fans, it's less work for you and it gets you more supporters:
  • Tingles is actively encouraging users to become your supporters so you don't have to. On Patreon all the promotion efforts are on you.
  • Tingles provides most of the rewards for your paying supporters. On Patreon you have to provide all the rewards for your supporters yourself.
  • Becoming a supporter on Tingles is as easy as one tap. This is so much easier than Patreon where users have to go to a different site, create a new account, add their credit card and so on.
  • Tingles offers better rewards for paying supporters. Being a supporter on Patreon doesn't unlock any benefits on YouTube where most of the users still watch your videos. Because Tingles is one unified platform it can offer much better benefits to your paying supporters.
Do I need to upload videos to Tingles?
No. You keep posting your videos on YouTube as normal and they will also show on Tingles automatically. When you join Tingles you get access to your Tingles Partners website where you get an overview of your presence on Tingles. For exclusive and early access videos see below.
What are exclusive videos?
Exclusive videos mean videos that are only available on Tingles and not available on YouTube. They are locked for free users and only accessible to your paying supporters. It's your choice if you want to have them, but they're a great way to convince more of your fans to become paying supporters.
What is early access to new videos?
Early access to new videos means new videos become available on Tingles some time before they become available on YouTube. You can think of these videos as temporarily exclusive. It.s advised to leave them exclusive for one week before making them available on other platforms. Early access videos are locked for free users and only accessible to your paying supporters. It's your choice if you want to have them, but they're a great way to convince more of your fans to become paying supporters.
Can I use my Patreon exclusive videos as exclusive videos on Tingles?
Yes.
How do I add exclusive and early access videos to Tingles?
To add an exclusive or early access video, simply upload it to YouTube and mark it as unlisted. You will then be able to add it as an exclusive video through Tingles Partners website.
Is there a fee to join Tingles?
No. Being on Tingles is free.

We finance the platform by keeping a percentage of the revenue. Our partners receive 70% of the net-revenue and Tingles keeps 30% that goes into platform maintenance and development costs.

How do I get paid?
Payments are sent out monthly. We support PayPal and bank wire transfer.
What if I want to delete my account?
You can delete your Tingles account anytime and all your content will be removed from Tingles. To delete your account click the Delete Account button at the bottom of the Profile tab on Tingles Partners website. There is no penalty and you will be paid out your remaining earnings.
How do I start?
To join Tingles go to Tingles Partners website or join here.
I have additional questions, where can I contact you?
Email us at hello@gettingles.com.
