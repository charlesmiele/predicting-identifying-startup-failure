Idea Enthusiasts

Novel ideas fuel our entrepreneurial passion. With a breakthrough concept for women, Sprout launched in 2011 to focus on delivering a first-in-class, first-in-disease approval for female sexual dysfunction. Sprout is the owner of flibanserin, a novel, non-hormonal treatment that has shown promise addressing hypoactive sexual desire disorder, the most common form of female sexual dysfunction.
